Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 June 2026 · Last updated: 1 June 2026

Summary

VANDR is a fully on-device fitness app. We do not run our own servers, we do not collect your personal data, and we do not sell or share anything you do in the app. Almost everything you create — workouts, weight logs, body measurements, water intake, photos — stays on your iPhone or iPad and never leaves.

What we collect

Nothing personal is sent to us. VANDR does not run analytics, does not collect device identifiers, and does not transmit your workout data, weight, body measurements, or any other personal information to any server we operate.

VANDR stores the following only on your device:

  • Your profile (username, avatar, age, sex, goal, fitness level)
  • Your daily workout history, streak and XP
  • Your weight log and body measurement entries (PRO)
  • Your water intake log
  • Your notification preferences and times
  • Your purchased PRO entitlement (handled by Apple's StoreKit)

What we never collect

  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect or transmit photos you import as your avatar
  • We do not use cross-app advertising identifiers
  • We do not run third-party analytics, tracking, or attribution SDKs
  • We do not share or sell any data

Permissions we request

Notifications — to send the daily workout reminder and (PRO) hydration reminders at the times you set. You can disable these at any time in iOS Settings or inside the app.

Photo library / camera — only used when you tap to set a custom profile picture. The chosen photo stays on your device and is referenced by file path; it is never uploaded.

In-app purchases

VANDR PRO is a one-time $5 in-app purchase processed by Apple. We never see your payment information. Apple handles the transaction and tells our app whether you own PRO — that's the only thing we know. To restore a previous purchase on a new device, tap Restore Purchase inside the VANDR PRO screen.

Children's privacy

VANDR is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since we don't collect data from anyone, this is automatic.

Data deletion

Because all your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time:

  • In-app: Profile → App → Reset All Data wipes everything.
  • Or: uninstalling VANDR from your device permanently deletes all VANDR data.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and surface a notice in-app at next launch.
