What is VANDR?

VANDR is a daily fitness app that builds the habit through simple structure: three workout tasks a day, optional side quests for bonus XP, an XP-based level system, and a streak that tracks how consistently you show up. The hard part isn't planning the workout — it's doing it. VANDR keeps that part simple.

Features

15-level XP progression · streak tracking with grace days · workout history calendar · weight, BMI & body measurements tracking · hydration tracker · daily motivational quotes · 4 colour themes · share progress cards · custom activities (PRO) · full history (PRO) · training radar (PRO).
